George Carlin

Man who changed comedy

George Carlin performing in 2008
  • Born May 12, 1937
  • Joined the Air Force in 1954
  • Teamed up with Jack Burns in 1959
  • Moved to California in 1960 and recorded an album ‘Burns and Carlin at the Playboy Club Tonight’.
  • 1967, he released his debut solo album ‘Take-Offs and Put-Ons.'
  • ‘Class Clown’ was released in 1972
  • Released the albums ‘Carlin at Carnegie Hall’ (1982), ‘Carlin on Campus’ (1984), and ‘Playin’ with Your Head’ (1986)
  • In 1987, chosen to play a role in the comedy movie ‘Outrageous Fortune.’
  • ‘The George Carlin Show’ in 1993 with Sam Simom
  • His final comedy album ‘It’s Bad for Ya’ was out in 2008. It was recorded just months before his death.
